How to read this
Orientation

One plan, one enemy, one through line.

The plan runs on a single category (equipment intelligence) and a single enemy (guesswork). Every month blends three things on purpose: a manufacturer feature every other month, a thought-leadership piece, and a data or operator story. Two recurring segments run in the background: Anonymous Operator Tells All, and the ecofit Floor Index and State of the Gym Floor as the data franchise.

How to use it

Live planner: the interactive tool where you drag concepts into months, edit teasers, and leave feedback right on the cards.

This deck: the static, readable snapshot for circulation to Dave, Tim, and Phil.

Legend on the cards

  • Pillar chip: Thought Leadership, Operator Story, Partner Deep Dive, or Key Metric.
  • Ready chip: a Dave-voice draft is written and linked on the card in the planner.
  • Blocked chip: waiting on data traceability, consent, or a partner sign-off.

Working principles
Guardrails

The rules that keep this safe.

Data

Anonymized and verticalized data only, in anything public. No customer-identifiable data, ever. No cross-brand comparisons. Real-data reports run through Will and Sergey. The Floor Index and State of the Gym Floor depend on the anonymization pipeline; nothing scheduled to publish until that is confirmed.

Voice and review

Dave's voice on thought leadership, graded before publish. No em dashes in published copy, use periods, commas, colons, or parentheses. Nothing publishes without Will's review. Bill fills the hopper, filters, and serves drafts through the planner for approval.

Partners

Co-branded pieces need partner sign-off before publish. Manufacturer reports are anonymized public reports or private brand-specific reports on request; never cross-brand comparisons. Woven is a messaging partner: we send data to Woven, we do not consume it.

Vision AI

Product page kept broad and clearly marked beta while the feature set is in flux. Non-fitness verticals are a careful, later exploration; those markets have real competition unlike fitness.
