There's a question fitness operators have been asking for years, usually informally, usually after a member complains: which equipment is actually getting used?

Not which equipment looks good in the sales deck. Not which brand has the best warranty pitch. Which machines are members actually gravitating toward when they have a choice?

At EcoFit, we've been answering that question with data for over a decade. Across thousands of fitness facilities, we track equipment usage at the session level — who's on what machine, for how long, at what time of day, and how that behavior shifts over weeks and months.

And one pattern shows up so consistently that it's stopped being a surprise.

"When Woodway treadmills share a floor with competitor brands, members choose Woodway at a disproportionate rate. The margin isn't close."

Why This Partnership Exists

EcoFit and Woodway have worked together for years. That relationship deepened significantly when Woodway developed their ProSmart connected treadmill line — a console-level system designed to bring real-time intelligence to the most demanding cardio equipment in the industry.

The question we asked together was straightforward: what if operators didn't need to install anything to get that intelligence? What if the data just flowed — the moment the machine powered on?

That's what the EcoFit x Woodway ProSmart integration delivers.

EcoFit software is now embedded natively in Woodway ProSmart connected consoles. There's no external hardware to install, no sensors to calibrate, no IT project to manage. The integration activates globally across any connected ProSmart product. From day one, operators get a live feed of usage data and equipment diagnostics flowing directly into EcoFit.

What Operators Get

The integration delivers three distinct layers of intelligence:

Usage Analytics

Every session on a ProSmart treadmill is captured and surfaced in EcoFit's operator dashboard. Peak usage windows, average session length, machine-level utilization rates, and comparative performance across your full equipment floor. The kind of data that used to require a manual audit now updates in real time.

Proactive Maintenance

ProSmart consoles transmit live error codes directly to EcoFit. When something is wrong — or about to be wrong — your service team knows before a member reports it. Turnaround time on service calls drops. Equipment uptime improves. The member experience stays intact.

Competitive Floor Intelligence

For operators running mixed-brand floors, the integration makes the comparison explicit. You'll see how Woodway utilization stacks up against every other piece of cardio equipment you own. In our experience across thousands of facilities, that data tends to tell a consistent story.

The Bigger Picture

The fitness industry has spent years talking about connected equipment. Most of that conversation has centered on the member-facing experience — screens, streaming classes, digital coaching.

What's been underbuilt is the operator side. The data that helps a facility director make better decisions about their floor, their maintenance budget, their equipment purchases, and their member retention strategy.

That's the gap EcoFit was built to close. And it's the reason this partnership with Woodway matters — not just as a technical integration, but as a statement about what equipment intelligence should look like when it's done right.

The best treadmill in the world is now generating the best data. The only question is whether you're using it.
