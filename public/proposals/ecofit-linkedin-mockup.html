Woodway treadmills are the most-used piece of cardio equipment on floors where multiple brands compete.

We know this because EcoFit has been tracking it for years across thousands of facilities.

The data doesn't lie. When you put a Woodway next to a competitor on the same floor, members vote with their feet. Woodway wins. Every time.

So when Woodway came to us about integrating EcoFit directly into their ProSmart connected consoles, the answer was obvious.

…see more

Starting now, EcoFit is embedded natively in Woodway ProSmart treadmills. No external sensors. No installation overhead. The moment a ProSmart console powers on, it's feeding real-time data directly into EcoFit — usage patterns, session data, and live error codes that trigger proactive maintenance before a machine goes down.

For operators, this means three things:

→You know exactly how your Woodway treadmills are being used, by whom, and when — down to the session.
→Your service team gets notified before equipment fails, not after a member reports it.
→You have the data to prove what your members already know: Woodway outperforms everything else on the floor.

This is what equipment intelligence looks like when the best hardware and the best analytics platform build something together.

If you want to see what your Woodway treadmills are telling you — and what you've been missing — we'd love to show you.
