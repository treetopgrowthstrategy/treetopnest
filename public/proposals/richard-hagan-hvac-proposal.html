01 — Overview

What We're Building

Before a single dollar of ad spend is deployed, the foundation needs to be right. This engagement begins with a thorough audit of the competitive landscape, the existing funnel, the ad account infrastructure, and the media mix — so that every tactical decision that follows is grounded in real data from your specific market.

The up-front phase delivers a complete strategic picture. The ongoing retainer converts that picture into consistent, managed execution — with a clear owner accountable to results.

02 — Phase One

Up-Front Audit & Setup

A one-time engagement to map the terrain, evaluate the client's current position, and build the infrastructure required to compete effectively in the North Georgia market.

01 / 06
Competitor Funnel Analysis
Audit 3 to 5 competing HVAC operators in the region — ads, landing pages, offers, and follow-up sequences. Identify patterns, gaps, and angles the client can exploit.
02 / 06
Existing Ad Spend & Funnel Review
Evaluate the client's current ad account performance — spend, creative, targeting, and landing page — to identify what's working, what's wasted, and what to preserve going forward.
03 / 06
Ad Account Setup & Conversion Tracking
Configure ad accounts properly across platforms, install pixel and tag infrastructure, and verify conversion tracking end-to-end from ad click to destination page event — no blind spots.
04 / 06
Creative Audit
Review all existing creative assets — static, video, and copy — against what's performing in the HVAC vertical today. Deliver a clear assessment of what to retire, refresh, or scale.
05 / 06
Keyword Volume Research
Research monthly search volume for primary HVAC terms specific to North Georgia — service-based, brand-based, and emergency intent — to inform Google campaign structure and budget priority.
06 / 06
Media Spend Allocation
Deliver a recommended budget split across Meta and Google — grounded in keyword demand, audience size, and competitive pressure — so every dollar is allocated before launch, not after.
