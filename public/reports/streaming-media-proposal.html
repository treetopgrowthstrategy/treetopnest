The Case for Streaming Now

The shift from linear television to connected TV has reached an inflection point. Nielsen data confirms streaming surpassed 47% of all U.S. TV watch time in mid-2025, while cable has fallen below 27%. Advertising dollars are following audiences at an accelerating rate — and inventory pricing has improved significantly as major platforms expanded their ad-supported tiers.

Unlike linear TV, streaming advertising allows geographic targeting to the DMA or ZIP code level, frequency capping at the household level, and real-time performance measurement. Every dollar your campaign spends can be directed precisely at viewers in your target markets — with zero waste outside them.

Window is open. CPMs that once averaged $40+ have moderated to $15–35 as Netflix, Amazon, and Disney+ expanded ad tiers. This creates a favorable combination: premium reach at increasingly competitive rates, with targeting precision linear TV cannot match.

Strategic Recommendation

A phased approach minimizes risk, validates creative before scaling, and ensures each budget threshold (including Amazon's $10K self-serve minimum) is crossed on the strength of proven performance data.

  • 01
    Months 1–3
    Launch on Hulu + Paramount+
    Run both platforms simultaneously across all three DMAs. Budget: $10K–$20K/month. Validate creative, identify which markets and audience segments respond best, establish baseline Video Completion Rate and engagement benchmarks. Produce at least two creative variants from launch.
  • 02
    Months 4–6
    Add Amazon Prime Video
    Bring Amazon DSP online at $10K/month minimum. Leverage Amazon's unmatched first-party purchase data for audience targeting. Optimize Hulu and Paramount+ based on Phase 1 performance — shift budget toward top-performing markets and creative.
  • 03
    Month 7+
    Netflix Expansion + Premium Formats
    When budget and performance support it, explore Netflix via programmatic DSP (The Trade Desk or DV360) at $50K+ commitment. Add Paramount+ sports inventory (NFL on CBS, UFC) for seasonally relevant high-attention placements. Introduce pause ads and interactive formats as they mature in 2026.
To build the full media plan, we need your target audience profile (age, income, interests), campaign objective (brand awareness vs. conversion), and total media budget range. That unlocks flight dates, CPM bids, impression forecasts, and creative specifications for each platform and market.
