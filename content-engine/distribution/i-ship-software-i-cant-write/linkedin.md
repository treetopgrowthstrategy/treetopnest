I ran a HubSpot partner agency for twelve years.

Then I built my own CRM to replace it. And I cannot write a line of code.

For years I watched people buy HubSpot like they were buying a name. The brand, the story, as if the software would log in and do the work for them. It never does. No software does.

And the pricing was a ladder that only climbed. A starter tier that could barely do anything, then 800 a month, then 1,200, for tools they used a fraction of, on contracts they could not leave.

At some point I stopped seeing a tool and started seeing a toll. So I built my own. Deal tracking, outbound, exactly what I needed and nothing I did not. It replaced Pipedrive, HubSpot, and Instantly.

Here is the part that surprised me. The hard part was never the code. It was the requirements. Knowing exactly what I wanted, writing it down clearly enough that a capable model could build it, and knowing it when I saw it.

Syntax got cheap. Specification and judgment got scarce.

I did not learn to code. I learned to deploy intelligence, and own my stack instead of renting it.

What is one tool you are renting that you suspect you could replace?
