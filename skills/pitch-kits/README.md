# Treetop pitch kits

Internal outreach artifacts — drafted to be copy-paste-and-customize ready. Not published on the public site. Updated as new outlets, podcasts, or angles emerge.

## What's in here

| File | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `podcast-pitch-kit.md` | Master podcast pitch kit. Bill's bio, sample angles by show type, fill-in-the-blank pitch templates. |
| `fitness-podcast-pitch-list.md` | Specific fitness-industry podcasts ranked by fit, with show host, contact path, and tailored pitch angle for each. |
| `fitness-publication-pitch-list.md` | Fitness industry trade outlets (Athletic Business, Club Industry, IHRSA, etc.) with editor contact paths and pitch angles. |
| `b2b-mid-market-pitch-list.md` | B2B mid-market journalists and outlets (Inc, Fast Company, HBR, etc.) with topic angles. |
| `franchise-corporate-pitch-list.md` | Franchise corporate office contacts (multi-unit operator audiences) — vendor evaluator + thought leadership angles. |

## Usage

These are **first drafts**. Every outgoing pitch should be customized per outlet/host — referencing a specific recent episode or article, naming the host or editor, and aligning the angle to their audience. Generic copy-paste sends are worse than no send.

## Tracking

When pitches go out, log to: `~/Documents/treetop-outreach-log.csv` (date, outlet, contact, angle pitched, response status, notes).

## Maintenance

Refresh quarterly — outlets and editors change; podcast guest cadence changes; topic angles evolve with the publishing cycle.
