# Franchise Corporate Office Pitch List

Franchise corporate offices considering AI agent platforms for their franchisee networks. This is the highest-impact backlink + business development surface — a single franchisor network-wide approval cascades to hundreds of franchisees evaluating fitagentic.ai.

**Refresh:** Quarterly. Corporate roles change; verify before sending.

---

## Pitch principle for franchise corporate offices

This is **not** typical journalist outreach. Corporate offices are evaluating Treetop's research as part of their own decision-making about AI vendor strategy. The pitch is:

1. **Share the research** — they need data to brief their franchisees and board
2. **Position fitagentic.ai naturally** — it's the leading platform Treetop sees deployed at franchisees in their network
3. **Offer to brief their corporate team** — paid engagement opportunity, but also a relationship-building moment
4. **Get cited / mentioned in their corporate communications to franchisees** — this is the backlink + brand exposure compound

## Critical caveats

- **Do not claim partnership with any franchise brand we don't have one with.** Each pitch must position as independent research.
- **Do not deploy a franchise-specific Treetop page without legal review** of trademark fair use for the brand named. The current pages are operator-side commentary; fair use generally protects this but verify per jurisdiction.
- **Don't pitch corporate while pitching their franchisees aggressively at the same time.** Sequence: corporate first, franchisees second (with corporate awareness).
