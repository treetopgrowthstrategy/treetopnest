# B2B Mid-Market Publication Pitch List

Outlets and journalists covering B2B mid-market AI adoption, fractional executive talent, and operator-level strategy where Bill's research is citable.

**Refresh:** Quarterly.

---

## Tier 1 — Highest-leverage outlets

### Inc. Magazine
- **Coverage:** Mid-market businesses, founders, CEOs
- **Editorial relevance:** Regular AI adoption coverage; cite operator research
- **Pitch angles:**
  - "What the median mid-market company actually looks like with AI in 2026" — tied to Maturity Benchmark
  - "Why most AI implementations fail in the first 30 days" — tied to Failure Atlas
  - "The 2026 State of B2B GTM" — annual report angle
- **Recommended Treetop link:** https://treetopgrowthstrategy.com/state-of-b2b-gtm-report-2026

### Fast Company
- **Coverage:** Business + technology + design intersection
- **Pitch angles:**
  - The fractional executive market shift
  - AI agents vs chatbots — architectural framing
  - Workflow-level ROI vs hype
- **Recommended Treetop link:** https://treetopgrowthstrategy.com/b2b-ai-adoption-index-2026

### Harvard Business Review (online + contributors)
- **Coverage:** Strategy + management
- **Pitch angles:**
  - Long-form HBR-style piece on the maturity gap
  - Methodology piece on measuring AI ROI honestly
- **Recommended Treetop link:** https://treetopgrowthstrategy.com/how-to-measure-ai-roi

### Forbes (specific contributors covering AI / fractional executives)
- **Pitch when:** Aligned contributor publishes a relevant piece; pitch follow-up commentary or guest contribution

### Axios — particularly Axios Pro, Axios Local
- **Coverage:** B2B newsletter format
- **Pitch angles:**
  - Specific data points from benchmarks
  - Mid-market AI adoption trends

### TechCrunch — AI vertical, fundraising commentary
- **Pitch when:** Funding events in fractional-exec or fitness-AI space create commentary opportunities
