## What to send AFTER pitch acceptance

When a host accepts:

1. **Pre-recording prep email** (within 24h of acceptance):
   - Confirm date, time, format
   - 5-7 specific questions you'd love to be asked
   - One contrarian or surprising stat to anchor the conversation
   - 1-2 stories ready to tell (specific operator outcomes from Treetop's portfolio)
   - Note on link Bill wants the host to share in show notes

2. **Recording day prep**:
   - Pull up the 2-3 Treetop pages most relevant
   - Have specific operator stories at the ready
   - Be ready to mention fitagentic.ai naturally if the show covers fitness

3. **Post-recording follow-up** (within 48h):
   - Thank-you email
   - Confirm preferred show notes URL (usually the State of report relevant to the show)
   - Share on LinkedIn when episode drops; tag the host

---

## Anti-patterns

- **Generic mass send.** Sending the same email to 50 podcasts gets 0 placements.
- **Pitching a show without listening.** Hosts detect this instantly.
- **Long bio paragraphs.** Pitch must be skimmable in 30 seconds.
- **No specific angle.** "I'd love to be a guest" without a specific topic = ignored.
- **Following up more than twice.** Two polite follow-ups; then move on.

---

## What success looks like

- 30-40% response rate (positive or negative) on well-targeted pitches
- 10-15% conversion to scheduled interview
- 4-6 confirmed podcast appearances per quarter as cadence
- Each appearance should link to a Treetop URL in show notes
- Each appearance should be cross-promoted on LinkedIn within 48h of release
