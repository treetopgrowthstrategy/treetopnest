Core Capabilities

Built for operators who manage real complexity.

Six core modules that cover the full lifecycle of every piece of equipment in your network — from the day it arrives to the day it leaves the floor.

Full Inventory Management
Track brand, model, condition, purchase date, and warranty for every cardio and strength piece in your network. Full edit capabilities, archiving, and multi-location organization. Always know exactly what you own and where it is.
Live Equipment Status
Know which units are running, which need attention, and which are down — across every location, in real time. Stop relying on member complaints as your early warning system. Get ahead of issues before they hit the floor.
Custom PM Scheduling
Usage-based or calendar-based maintenance notifications delivered automatically to your team. Catch problems before they become member complaints. Build a maintenance culture that prevents, not just reacts.
Service & Task Tracking
Create and assign service tasks to your team. Track complete history for every machine. Log work orders, technician visits, parts replaced, and resolution time. Keep every machine in working order with a clear paper trail.
Equipment Transfer & Storage
Move equipment between locations or into storage. Track its complete history wherever it goes. Know the full lifecycle of every asset — from original deployment to transfer to eventual retirement — across your entire portfolio.
Analytic Reporting
Self-serve reports on usage, availability, saturation, and peak periods — updated automatically. Or request custom equipment studies from the Ecofit analytics team for deeper decisions on procurement, rotation, and club modeling.
