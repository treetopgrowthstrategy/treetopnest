The Integration

Two native ways to connect Woodway. Zero rip-and-replace.

Whether your Woodway treadmills shipped last month or seven years ago, Ecofit has a clean path to the data. No new hardware on the floor you don't need.

1
CSAFE Data Port + Ecofit Tracker
For any Woodway unit on your floor, connected or not. The Ecofit tracker plugs straight into the CSAFE port (Communication Specifications for Fitness Equipment), the universal data standard Woodway and every major brand support. It pulls usage directly from the machine and transmits it over Ecofit's own long-range wireless network to the gateway, then up to the cloud for processing.
Best for:
Existing Woodway fleet Non-connected units Mixed-age floors
A 15-year-engineered wireless protocol with longer range than low-power Bluetooth: built when no connectivity solution existed, still the most stable way to network a full floor.
2
Woodway Cloud → Ecofit Cloud
For Woodway's connected consoles. When the treadmill is activated on your facility network, its data flows up to the Woodway cloud, and Ecofit's direct cloud tie-in pulls it straight through to your dashboard. No tracker, no sensor, no install labor. It's the lightest-touch way to connect new Woodway equipment.
Best for:
New connected consoles Lower install cost No added hardware
Saves on hardware and installation. The client pays only a small data fee, often bundled directly into the Woodway service.
