How It Works

Up and running in days, not months.

1
Connect
Ecofit sensors attach to your equipment or pull directly from manufacturer consoles via native API integrations. No IT department required. No servers to configure. For most compatible equipment, it's plug-in simple.
2
Sync
Data flows automatically to the Ecofit cloud platform. No manual entry, no spreadsheets, no periodic exports. Everything updates in real time — usage events, status changes, error codes, and session data — continuously and automatically.
3
See
Usage, status, events, and analytics appear in your Ecofit dashboard within minutes of installation. Your floor comes to life — every asset visible, every session counted, every issue surfaced before your members find it first.
